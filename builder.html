  • Custom Design

    Long time area residents, Larry Brown and his son, John Brown have been premier builders of luxury shoreline homes for over 35 years. They have established a reputation of working closely with buyers in every step of the building process. Each home is custom built to the buyer’s specifications with the highest quality craftsmanship. There are many plans from which to choose or buyers make use of the builder’s custom architectural services for a home designed to their exact specifications. Kelly Brown heads the Bellevue Construction office in Madison and works directly with buyers in their selections and coordinates appointments with vendors and suppliers.

  • Signature Amenities

    With graceful driveways and enchanting silhouettes, the Browns’ homes enhance their natural surroundings rather than intrude upon it. They use timeless features such as spacious front porches with detailed railings, interesting roof lines and outstanding trim work framing large transom and palladian windows. Signature amenities are displayed in their homes such as nine foot ceilings, two staircases, rich crown moldings, columns separating the designer kitchens from the family rooms, vaulted ceilings, skylights, french doors, large bonus rooms over a 3 car garage, and if possible, a walkout basement.

  • Past Projects

    Larry and John have built many subdivisions in the area. They include Neck River Farms and Kelsey Court in Madison, The Woodlands, Bearhouse Hill Estates and Franklin’s Way in Guilford, and Chitttenden Meadows, Meadow View Estates, Clarkson Crossing and the Ridges of Deer Lake in Killingworth. They also have done a number of custom homes along the shoreline.
