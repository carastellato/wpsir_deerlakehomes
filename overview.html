The Ridges at Deer Lake in Killingworth, CT

The Ridges at Deer Lake, is another luxury subdivision by Bellevue Construction, conveniently located just one mile from the Madison town line and minutes from highways and shopping.

Bellevue Construction offers custom home packages starting at just $499,000 and situated on 31 wooded lots ranging from 2 to over 6 acres with expansive views of the surrounding countryside. There are acres of open space providing residents with privacy and solitude and the neighboring state forest offers miles of walking and horse trails. Great care has been taken to preserve the natural beauty of the land highlighted with seemlingly endless stone walls, rivers, ponds, and mature trees. Nearby Deer Lake is a great recreational area for boating, swimming, and fishing. Hammonaset State Park with miles of beach is only 8 miles away.
